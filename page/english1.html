MY COUNTRY

My country is Bangladesh. Bangladesh is a Southeast Asian country. It is small country but huge population. It gains independence twice. Once from Britain in 1947 and again from Pakistan in 1971. Dhaka is the capital of this country. More than 160 million people live in our country. Most of them are Muslims. Others are Hindus, Buddhists and Christians. Remaining people are doctors, engineers, teachers, businessmen, jobbers etc.All communities of people live here in happily and peacefully. National dress for men is pajama and Punjabi and for women is saree. Our country has six seasons in a year. The seasons are Summer, Monsoon, Autumn, Hemant, Winter and Spring. It is a riverine country. There are many rivers in our country. The main rivers are Padma, Jamuna and Meghna. Natural calamities like floods, cyclones and droughts hit the country every year. The climate of our country is warm and humid and the land is flat and fertile. So every year a lot of crops are grown here.Paddy, tea, jute, wheat etc. are our main crops. Bangladesh is a country of customs and traditions. Because each ethnic group living here has its own customs. For example, Eid for Muslims, Puja Parvana for Hindus, Buddhist Purnima for Buddhists and Big Day for Christians. One of the big events in our culture is called Bengali New Year. All Bengalis regardless of caste and religion participate in this festival of Pahela Boishakh. Bangladesh is a country which contains full of natural beauties. Bangladesh is our pride.We are really proud of our country.
